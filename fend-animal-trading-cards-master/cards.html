Cheetah

The fastest land animal in the world, its body has evolved for speed, with long legs, an elongated spine, adapted claws to grip the ground and a long tail for balance.

  • Speed: 112km/h in just three seconds
  • Life Span: 10-12 years
  • Population: 7,100
  • Weight: 110-140 punds
